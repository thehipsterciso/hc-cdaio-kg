# Stage 6 — Technology, Compute, and Platform Reality

*What does the technology estate actually constrain — and what cannot be changed without breaking something else?*

**Purpose.** Map technology as a constraint system — compute control, hyperscaler dependency, platform coupling, vendor lock-in, and technical debt as economic drag. Not a capability inventory. A constraint inventory.

**Produces.** Analysis of compute and data center control, cloud dependency and exit fiction, platform criticality and change fragility, vendor lock-in mechanics, technical debt economics, and technology estate coherence.

**Prerequisites.** Stages 1–5 validated and passed.

**Failure condition.** If the analysis cannot demonstrate that technology constraints materially limit business optionality, change velocity, or cost flexibility, Stage 6 has confused capability with constraint.
