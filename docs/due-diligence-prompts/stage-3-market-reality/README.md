# Stage 3 — Market Reality, External Pressure, and Competitive Force

*What external forces constrain this enterprise's revenue durability, margins, control, and optionality — and can the enterprise actually respond?*

**Purpose.** Determine how external market dynamics — competitive structure, customer behavior, pricing physics, platform dependency, and shock sensitivity — create constraints that the enterprise cannot unilaterally resolve.

**Produces.** Analysis of market power distribution, competitive threat vectors, customer switching physics, pricing signaling dynamics, platform dependency risk, and external shock sensitivity.

**Prerequisites.** Stages 1–2 validated and passed.

**Failure condition.** If external pressure cannot be shown to materially affect revenue durability, margins, or control, Stage 3 fails.
