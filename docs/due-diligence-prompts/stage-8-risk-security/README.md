# Stage 8 — Risk, Security, Privacy, and Regulatory Constraints

*What can go wrong that the organization cannot prevent, and what regulatory or compliance constraints limit how it can respond?*

**Purpose.** Map the risk and security constraint surface — cyber control failures, privacy and data rights obligations, regulatory enforcement exposure, data sovereignty immutability, and governance power dynamics that determine who can actually make risk decisions.

**Produces.** Analysis of cybersecurity control failure surface, privacy and use-limitation reality, regulatory enforcement and intervention risk, data residency constraints, and governance power and risk escalation dynamics.

**Prerequisites.** Stages 1–7 validated and passed.

**Failure condition.** If the analysis cannot demonstrate that risk, security, or regulatory constraints materially limit operational or strategic optionality, Stage 8 has underestimated the constraint environment.
