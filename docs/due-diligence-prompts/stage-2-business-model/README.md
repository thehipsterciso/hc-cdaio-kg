# Stage 2 — Business Model, Value Creation, and Economic Flow Reality

*How does this enterprise actually create, capture, and sustain economic value — and where does that mechanism break?*

**Purpose.** Map the real economics of the business — not the investor narrative, not the pitch deck, but how revenue actually flows, where costs actually land, and where the model contradicts itself.

**Produces.** Analysis of revenue engine mechanics, cost structure physics, end-to-end value flow friction, customer economic dependence, partner/channel asymmetry, and internal business model contradictions.

**Prerequisites.** Stage 1 validated and passed.

**Failure condition.** If the analysis cannot identify at least one material contradiction between the stated business model and observable economic behavior, Stage 2 has not looked hard enough.
