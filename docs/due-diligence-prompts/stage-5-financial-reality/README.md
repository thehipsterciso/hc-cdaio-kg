# Stage 5 — Financial Reality, Capital Constraints, and Control Under Stress

*What happens to the financials under stress — and where does the capital structure create constraints that strategy cannot override?*

**Purpose.** Discover financial reality under pressure. Not reported numbers — the quality of revenue signals, the physics of cost behavior, the real liquidity position, covenant constraints on control, and internal economic contradictions.

**Produces.** Analysis of revenue quality and signal integrity, margin behavior under stress, liquidity accessibility, debt covenant mechanics, capital allocation flexibility, and financial coherence contradictions.

**Prerequisites.** Stages 1–4 validated and passed.

**Failure condition.** If the analysis cannot identify at least one material gap between reported financial metrics and observable economic behavior, Stage 5 was too trusting.
