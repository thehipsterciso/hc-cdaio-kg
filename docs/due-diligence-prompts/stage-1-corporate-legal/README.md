# Stage 1 — Corporate, Legal, and Structural Reality

*What is this entity, who actually controls it, and what structural constraints exist that no strategy can override?*

**Purpose.** Discover the legal, ownership, and structural reality of the target organization. Not what the org chart says — what the corporate filings, ownership chains, contractual obligations, and jurisdictional requirements actually enforce.

**Produces.** Validated findings on legal existence, ownership chains, economic entitlement, structural debt from corporate history, change-of-control constraints, and jurisdictional compliance locks.

**Prerequisites.** Stage 0 constitution accepted.

**Failure condition.** If the analysis cannot demonstrate that structural constraints materially affect control, optionality, or transaction execution, Stage 1 fails.
