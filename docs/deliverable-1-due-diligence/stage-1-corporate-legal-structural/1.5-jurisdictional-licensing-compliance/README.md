**Search Outcome:** HYPOTHESIS STRONGLY SUPPORTED - Consolidation is legally and operationally impossible. Multi-jurisdictional structure is PERMANENT requirement for serving regulated customers across geographies.
**Claim Type:** `INFERENCE`  

**Evidence Sources:**

  - China Cybersecurity Law: In-country data storage mandatory
