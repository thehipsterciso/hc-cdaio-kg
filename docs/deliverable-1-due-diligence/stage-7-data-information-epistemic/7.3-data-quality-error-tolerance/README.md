**Data Domain:** Deal profitability—CRM opportunity value vs actual margin reality  

**Common Error Types:**
  - CRM opportunity value overstates deal profitability by 13-15 percentage points (Private Cloud -13% margin, Public Cloud -3% margin indicates systematic entry optimism)
  - Cost estimates at CRM entry incomplete or optimistic—delivery complexity, customization effort, operational overhead underestimated
  - Revenue assumptions invalid—pricing holds, customer doesn't request scope changes, renewal occurs at assumed rate
  - Profitability determination lagged—deal profitability known only after delivery execution, retrospective margin calculation months after booking

**Tolerance Threshold:** EXPLICIT THRESHOLD DISCOVERED Q4 2024: CEO tolerance ends when segment margins negative double-digits (-13% Private Cloud). Walk away policy Q4 2024 reactive recognition after prolonged tolerance (margin erosion visible Q1-Q3 2024 preceding policy). IMPLICIT THRESHOLD PRIOR: Tolerated indefinitely while aggregate corporate margins positive—segment-level losses masked in corporate aggregate allowing prolonged tolerance. Tolerance duration: Months-to-years per deal (CRM entry to profitability discovery lag), quarters-to-years enterprise-wide (margin erosion trending negative before intervention).

**Who Accepts The Risk:** RISK DISTRIBUTED: Sales benefits from CRM optimism through compensation (bookings-based incentive Stage 4.4), externalizes margin risk to delivery and finance. Delivery discovers cost reality post-signature, absorbs margin erosion through under-resourcing or losses (authority over delivery execution, no authority over deal acceptance Stage 4.1). Finance absorbs aggregate margin deterioration, manages investor expectations through conservative external adjustment (but adjustment insufficient to prevent -13%/-3% segment performance). CEO ultimately forced to intervene reactively when erosion severe (walk away policy, sales refresh Q3 2024). Customers indirectly absorb risk through service quality degradation when deals under-resourced to preserve margin.

**Business Or Regulatory Consequence:** BUSINESS CONSEQUENCES MATERIAL: Revenue rejection (walk away policy rejects unprofitable deals/renewals reducing revenue), sales force instability (Q3 2024 refresh, talent turnover Stage 4.5), resource misallocation (hiring and capacity planning based on inflated pipeline value), margin compression threatening debt covenants ($1.3B debt Stage 5, negative cash flow from operations Q2 2024). REGULATORY CONSEQUENCES POTENTIAL: If CRM pipeline materially overstates viability and used for investor guidance, forecast accuracy questioned by investors/SEC. No regulatory action visible in evidence—but investor credibility risk material.

**Touch Test What Breaks If Zero Tolerance Enforced:** Zero tolerance = profitability validation required at CRM entry before opportunity accepted. (5) Deal flexibility—profitability validation at entry assumes static requirements, but customer scope changes post-signature common, pre-validation effort wasted. CEO walk away policy is PARTIAL zero tolerance (reactive filter post-booking) not FULL zero tolerance (proactive validation pre-entry)—full enforcement breaks sales model.

**Common Error Types:**
